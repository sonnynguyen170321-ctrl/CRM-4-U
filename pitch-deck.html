The Problem

SDR teams are drowning
in tool chaos

📊

Scattered Lead Data

Lead lists live in Excel sheets. Each SDR tracks their own pipeline differently. No single source of truth — data integrity breaks the moment a rep joins or leaves.

🔀

Multi-Tool Fragmentation

Gmail, Outlook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, VoIP — all separate. SDRs context-switch constantly, losing momentum and dropping follow-ups between the gaps.

🚨

Zero Visibility for Managers

Floor Managers can't see which reps are overdue, which leads are stalled, or how sequences are performing — until it's too late to course-correct.

⏱️

Manual Sequence Tracking

Follow-up cadences live in people's heads or spreadsheets. Missed steps mean missed opportunities — and there's no way to know until a lead goes cold.

🔄

Repetitive Manual Work

SDRs retype the same outreach messages daily. No template library. No merge fields. Every email and LinkedIn message is composed from scratch.

📉

No Client Reporting

Generating client performance reports requires manually aggregating data from multiple sources. Hours of work for a single weekly update.

The Solution

One platform.
Every channel.
Full visibility.

🎯

Unified Lead Pipeline

Kanban board and list view with drag-and-drop stage management. Every lead, every stage, every SDR — one source of truth.

⚡

Automated Sequence Engine

Build multi-step, multi-channel cadences once. The platform tracks where every lead is and fires tasks automatically.

📈

Real-Time Manager Dashboard

Activity leaderboards, overdue alerts, pipeline funnels, and one-click client report exports.
